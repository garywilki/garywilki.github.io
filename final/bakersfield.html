Bakersfield!

Bakersfield is currently one of the fastest growing economic systems in the country. Sitting in the heart of Kern County, California, Bakersfield is a hub for both agriculture and energy production. We're known for being the most productive oil-producing county in California as well as the 4th most productive agricultural county in the United States.
