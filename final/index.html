Join the Bakersfield Chamber of Commerce!

The Bakersfield Chamber of Commerce seeks to foster a thriving social and economic community in Kern County where people of all backgrounds may connect with each other through city events, and businesses and other organizations can reach out to customers across the county.

Through the continual dedicated effort of our board and the continued support of our beloved patrons, we aim to provide residents and visitors with the most enhanced one-stop service for our great city.
