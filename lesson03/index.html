Domain Name

A short, catchy, memorable domain name is vital for members of Kern county surrounding Bakersfield. We have chosen the available domain name: bfweather.com

Site Logo

We have chosen the wind sock as a logo.

Site Purpose

Bakersfield Weather aims to be a central site where members of Kern county, California may visit for up-to-the-hour updates on local events, politics, weather, and other topics pertinent to public interest. People should be able to quickly visit geographic divisions that are most relevant to them to find news local to their area. Content we expect to see here includes: weather, upcoming public events, news, and tourist attractions.
