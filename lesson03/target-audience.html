Target Audience

Bakersfield Weather

Our vision is that Bakersfield Weather will be a favorite stop for people on-the-go, frequently commuting, visitors, or those stuck at home and seeking connections with the community. In each case we want to provide not only quick access to weather and news but also suggestions for local stops.

Generally we will be targeting people 20s and older in the Bakersfield and surrounding cities. We expect mobile access at least 70% of the time.
